About us

Lubingu Innovation Systems is an indigenous established Zambian company that was duly registered on 8thMay,2019 in accordance with provisions of the Companies Act No.10 of 2017 and the Companies Regulations of 2018, Sections 270 and 272 of the Laws of Zambia, under registration number 320190006756. The company has been progressively growing since inception and hopes to grow stronger in the next few years through the principal of earning trust and confidence from our clients by providing outstanding, affordable, quality and sustainable services.

Auxillia Boarding House Terms and Conditions

Please read carefully.

House Rules
  • You must follow the House Rules.
  • Your guests must follow them too.
  • Breaking the rules could get your lease canceled.
Lease Period
  • The lease starts on the first day of the semester and ends on the last day of the same semester (or another agreed date).
  • It's a fixed-term lease. If you cancel early, you must still pay rent for the full period.
Rent
  • Rent is due monthly and the first instalment must be paid at least a day before the commencement of the semester.
  • Late payments will not be tolerated.
  • Monthly rent must be paid in full - no instalment.
Security Deposit
  • You must pay a refundable deposit before moving in. You won’t get access to the room until it’s paid.
  • The deposit will be returned after the lease ends; minus any money you owe or damage costs.
Use of Room
  • The room is for living only - not for business or other purposes.
  • No loud or disruptive behavior.
  • Follow the House Rules at all times.
Other Important Terms
  • No Subletting: You cannot rent out or share the bed space with others.
  • Electricity and Water: Utilities are included in the monthly rent. If you use too much, rent may increase.
  • Insurance: Don’t bring dangerous or pest-infested items that could raise insurance costs. The landlord insures the building - not your personal items.
  • Security: You must follow standard security rules or procedures implemented by the Boarding House.
  • Landlord’s Repairs: The landlord will fix structural parts like walls and roof. The landlord is not responsible for damage due to gross negligence.
  • Tenant’s Maintenance: You must keep the room and furniture clean and in good condition.
  • Left-Behind Property: You must take all your belongings when you move out. Anything left behind may be thrown away, and you will not claim it later.
  • Electrical Safety: Do not change or tamper with electrical systems. Use only standard appliances.
  • Room Damage: Do not drill holes or damage walls or ceilings. Do not make any changes without written permission.
  • Room Inspections: The landlord can inspect the room at any time with reasonable notice.
  • Keys: You will receive keys before moving in. Return all keys when you move out.
  • Destruction of the Building: If the building is destroyed and the room cannot be lived in, the lease shall be terminated.
  • If You Do Not Pay on Time: If you do not pay rent within 5 business days after a written warning, the landlord can cancel the lease, remove you from the room, and keep your deposit until all debts are paid.
  • Indemnity: The landlord is not responsible for injury, theft, or loss unless caused by gross negligence on the part of the landlord.
